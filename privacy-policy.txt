2. Permissions

Our App requires the following permissions to function:

    MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: This permission allows the App to manage files on your device's external storage. It is necessary for organizing and managing your e Books.

    READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: This permission allows the App to read files from your device's external storage. It is used to access your eBooks and display them within the App.

    WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: This permission allows the App to write files to your device's external storage. It is required for saving and updating eBooks on your device.

3. Data Collection and Use
